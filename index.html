Guidelines & Instructions

How It Works

Welcome to the platform! Here's how you can start earning:

  1. Go to the "Tasks" page to see available jobs.
  2. Each task costs 1 credit to start. You begin with 50 credits.
  3. Click "Start Task" to use a credit and unlock the task details and link.
  4. Follow the instructions and complete the task (e.g., leave a comment or review).
  5. Submit proof of your work (like a link to your comment) to complete the task and earn $0.10.

Review Writing Guidelines

To ensure your submissions are approved, please follow these simple rules:

Do:

  • Be specific. Mention details from the product, service, or video.
  • Maintain a positive and constructive tone.
  • Check your spelling and grammar before submitting.

Don't:

  • Do not use generic comments like "Nice video" or "Great product."
  • Do not use offensive language or spam.
  • Do not copy and paste reviews from other sources. Your work must be original.
