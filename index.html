How It Works

Welcome to the platform! Here's how you can start earning:

  1. Go to the "Tasks" page to see available jobs.
  2. Each task costs 1 credit to start. You begin with 50 credits.
  3. Click "Start Task" to use a credit and unlock the task details and link.
  4. Follow the instructions and complete the task (e.g., leave a comment or review).
  5. Submit proof of your work (like a link to your comment) to complete the task and earn $0.10.
